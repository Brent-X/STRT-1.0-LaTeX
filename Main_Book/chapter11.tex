% Chapter 11: Numerical Simulation and Lattice Models

The toroidal metamaterial lattices used in the QuTiP simulations benefit greatly from the higher-dimensional visualization techniques pioneered by Thomas F. Banchoff. His methods for rendering and interacting with 4D and higher tori provide the conceptual tools needed to design and interpret the 5.237 THz-driven time-crystal phases that stabilize the S-factor in STRT 1.0 (Banchoff 1996).
