% Chapter 8: Low-Power Spacetime Engineering

The resonance-induced negative-energy bubbles described in this chapter rely on toroidal metamaterial lattices. As Thomas F. Banchoff has demonstrated in his work on the visualization of higher-dimensional tori and Clifford tori (Banchoff 1996), such structures in 4D and higher dimensions can be rendered and understood through careful projection and interactive graphics. This geometric insight is essential for designing the physical lattices that realize the S-factor screening \(S \approx 0.618\) and negative-energy sandwich predicted by STRT 1.0.
